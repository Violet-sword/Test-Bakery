About Our Home Roasted Coffee

Welcome to Test Bakery's coffee corner, where we take pride in our commitment to quality and craftsmanship in every cup. At Test Bakery, we believe that great coffee starts with exceptional beans and a passion for roasting.

Our journey begins with carefully sourced green coffee beans from renowned regions around the world. From the lush highlands of Ethiopia to the sunny slopes of Colombia, each bean is selected for its unique flavor profile and quality.

Once in our hands, these beans undergo a meticulous roasting process right here in our bakery. Our small-batch roasting ensures that every batch is roasted to perfection, bringing out the nuanced flavors and aromas inherent in each bean. Whether you prefer a rich and robust espresso or a smooth and velvety latte, our freshly roasted coffee promises a delightful experience with every sip.

We invite you to explore our diverse selection of single-origin coffees and signature blends, each crafted to satisfy the discerning coffee enthusiast. Join us at Test Bakery and discover why our home-roasted coffee is a favorite among locals and coffee aficionados alike.
